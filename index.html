Gym Advice

Consistency is key when it comes to working out. Make a schedule and stick to it.

Mix up your routines to challenge your muscles and avoid boredom. Try new exercises and machines.

Stay hydrated before, during, and after your workout. Water is essential for proper bodily function.

Warm up before starting your workout. This helps to reduce the risk of injury and prepares your muscles.

Listen to your body. If you feel pain or discomfort, stop and rest. Consult a doctor if necessary.
